About

Independent Counsel.
Undivided Attention.

Lakshmi Sreedhar is an independent advocate based in Kochi, practising primarily before the High Court of Kerala and the Supreme Court of India. A graduate of the National University of Advanced Legal Studies (NUALS), Kochi, she has built a practice spanning constitutional litigation, corporate advisory, property law, and public interest matters — earning a reputation for meticulous preparation and principled advocacy.

With over nine years at the Bar, Lakshmi combines the agility of independent practice with the depth of experience typically found at larger firms. Every client receives her direct attention, from the first consultation through the final resolution.
