About

Independent counsel.
Undivided attention.

Lakshmi Sreedhar is an advocate based in Kerala. She graduated from the National University of Advanced Legal Studies (NUALS) in 2016 and has been practising since — appearing before the High Court of Kerala, the Supreme Court of India, and courts and forums across the country.

Her practice covers a wide range — constitutional and administrative law, civil and criminal litigation, corporate and commercial matters, matrimonial matters, intellectual property, media law, banking, consumer, arbitration, and more. She also takes up pro bono matters.

Every client works with Lakshmi directly.
